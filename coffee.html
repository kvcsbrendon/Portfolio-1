Expresso Dolce

A Southern Italian classic, Espresso Dolce elevates the bold intensity of espresso with a velvety side of whipped cream and the perfect amount of sweetness from sugar cubes. This refined yet indulgent coffee experience captures the essence of Italian café culture—with just a touch of luxury.

Preparation Time

2 Minutes

Make Time

3 Minutes

Serving Size

1 Shot

Bold Creamy Sweet Authentic

Instructions

Step 1 - Brew the Espresso

Prepare 1 shot (30ml) of espresso using an espresso machine, stovetop moka pot, or any preferred brewing method. Pour it into a small espresso cup.

Step 2 - Prepare the Cream

Spoon 2 tbsp of whipped cream into a small dish or directly onto the espresso if you prefer a creamier blend.

Step 3 - Serve with Sugar Cubes

Place 2 sugar cubes on the side of the espresso cup, allowing the drinker to sweeten their coffee as desired.

Step 4 - Enjoy Like an Italian

Sip the espresso as is, stir in sugar for a sweeter experience, or dip the sugar cubes into the coffee for a true Southern Italian ritual. Pair with the whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence.

Ingredients

1 shot (30ml) espresso

2 tbsp whipped cream

2 sugar cubes

Tools

Espresso machine or moka pot

Small espresso cup

Spoon for stirring
