Lasagna Texana

A bold, Southern-inspired twist on a classic Italian favorite. This hearty lasagna ditches ricotta for creamy cottage cheese and swaps traditional béchamel for a rich, smoky BBQ sauce. Layered with seasoned ground beef and ready-to-use pasta sheets, Lasagna Texana is a dish that’s guaranteed to impress—Some people might not approve, but your taste buds definitely will.

Preparation Time

15 Minutes

Total Time

40 Minutes

Serving Size

6-8 Servings

Garfield Hearty Cheese Comfort Food

Instructions

Step 1 - Preheat the Oven

Set your oven to 180°C (350°F) to ensure it’s ready for baking once the lasagna is assembled.

Step 2 - Prepare the Meat Filling

In a large skillet over medium heat, add 1 tbsp of oil and brown 500g of ground beef until fully cooked (about 5-7 minutes). Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste. Once done, remove from heat and mix in 1 cup of BBQ sauce until the beef is well coated.

Step 3 - Mix the Cottage Cheese Layer

In a bowl, combine 2 cups of cottage cheese with 1 beaten egg and ½ tsp salt. Stir until smooth and creamy.

Step 4 - Assemble the Lasagna

In a large baking dish, start layering:

  • Base Layer: Spread a thin layer of BBQ sauce at the bottom.
  • Pasta Sheets: Place a layer of store-bought lasagna sheets (no need to pre-boil).
  • Meat Layer: Evenly spread a portion of the BBQ-seasoned ground beef.
  • Cheese Layer: Add a layer of cottage cheese mixture.
  • Repeat: Continue layering pasta, beef, and cottage cheese until all ingredients are used.
  • Final Layer: Top with a generous layer of shredded mozzarella (1 ½ cups) and drizzle extra BBQ sauce over the top for a smoky finish.

Step 5 - Bake the Lasagna

Cover the dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 10 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

Step 6 - Let It Rest and Serve

Once out of the oven, let the lasagna sit for 10 minutes before slicing—this helps the layers set properly. Serve warm and enjoy your Lasagna Texana!

Ingredients

500g ground beef

1 tbsp oil

1 cup BBQ sauce (plus extra for topping)

2 cups cottage cheese

1 egg, beaten

½ tsp salt

½ tsp garlic powder

1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella

Store-bought lasagna sheets

Tools

Large skillet for cooking beef

Mixing bowl for cheese mixture

Baking dish

Spoon for spreading layers

Aluminum foil
