Spaghetti alla Wurstel

A true Italian street food classic, loved by those in the know. Perfectly boiled spaghetti is coated in a rich, vibrant ketchup sauce, complemented by the savory bite of sliced hot dogs. Simple, satisfying, and bursting with bold flavors, this dish is a must-try for any pasta enthusiast.

Preparation Time

5 Minutes

Make Time

15 Minutes

Serving Size

2-4 Servings

Pasta Hot Dogs Ketchup Street Food

Instructions

Step 1 - Cook the Spaghetti

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add 250g of spaghetti and cook according to package instructions (or a little longer for a softer texture). Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Once done, drain the spaghetti and return it to the pot.

Step 2 - Prepare the Hot Dogs

While the pasta is cooking, slice 2-3 hot dogs into thin rounds. If you prefer extra flavor, lightly pan-fry them over medium heat for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly browned and crispy.

Step 3 - Coat the Spaghetti in Ketchup

Return the drained spaghetti to the pot and add ½ cup of ketchup. Stir well to coat the noodles evenly. If you want a thinner sauce, add a splash of water.

Step 4 - Add the Hot Dogs

Toss in the sliced hot dogs and mix them into the spaghetti, ensuring even distribution. Cook for another 1-2 minutes on low heat to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 5 - Serve and Enjoy

Plate the spaghetti and garnish with grated Parmesan (if desired). Serve hot and enjoy your authentic Spaghetti alla Wurstel.

Ingredients

250g spaghetti

½ cup ketchup

2-3 hot dogs, sliced

1 tbsp oil (for frying hot dogs, optional)

Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Tools

Large pot for boiling pasta

Strainer for draining spaghetti

Pan for frying hot dogs (optional)

Wooden spoon for mixing
