Tiramisu Fragola

A delicate Italian dessert with a fruity twist, Tiramisu Fragola replaces traditional mascarpone with the light, airy texture of Cool Whip, while layers of sponge cake are infused with instant coffee and sweet strawberry jelly. Served chilled, this dessert offers a refreshing take on the beloved classic—perfect for any occasion.

Preparation Time

10 Minutes

Make Time

2 Hours

Serving Size

6-8 Servings

Sweet Fruity Coffee No-Bake

Instructions

Step 1 - Prepare the Coffee Mixture

Dissolve 2 tbsp of instant coffee granules in ½ cup of warm water. Set aside to cool.

Step 2 - Prepare the Sponge Cake Layers

Take 1 store-bought sponge cake and slice it into even layers (or use pre-cut cake slices). Dip each slice lightly into the cooled coffee mixture, making sure not to soak it completely.

Step 3 - Layer the Dessert

In a large dish or individual serving cups, layer the ingredients as follows:

  • Base Layer: A layer of coffee-soaked sponge cake.
  • Jelly Layer: Spread ¼ cup of strawberry jelly evenly over the cake.
  • Whipped Cream Layer: Add a thick layer of Cool Whip (about ½ cup per layer).
  • Repeat: Continue layering until all ingredients are used, finishing with a generous whipped cream topping.

Step 4 - Chill and Set

Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 5 - Serve and Enjoy

Before serving, dust the top lightly with cocoa powder or sprinkle extra instant coffee granules for a bold espresso touch. Serve chilled and enjoy your Tiramisu Fragola!

Ingredients

1 store-bought sponge cake

2 tbsp instant coffee granules

½ cup warm water

½ cup strawberry jelly

2 cups Cool Whip

Cocoa powder for garnish (optional)

Tools

Mixing bowl for coffee mixture

Knife for slicing sponge cake

Spoon or spatula for layering

Plastic wrap for chilling
