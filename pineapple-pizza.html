Authentic Napolian Pizza

An authentic Neapolitan pizza, crafted with a rich ketchup base and perfectly balanced with sweet pineapple and melted cheese. A true culinary masterpiece that brings a delightful harmony of flavors to every bite.

Preparation Time

20 Minutes

Total Time

20 Minutes

Serving Size

2-4 Servings

Pineapple Pizza Homemade Dinner

Instructions

Step 1 - Prepare the Dough

If using homemade pizza dough, lightly flour a clean surface and roll out the dough into a thin, even circle or rectangle, about ¼-inch thick. If using store-bought dough, let it sit at room temperature for 10 minutes before rolling to prevent shrinking. Transfer the rolled dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone lined with parchment paper.

Step 2 - Preheat the Oven

Set your oven to 220°C (425°F) and allow it to fully preheat. If using a pizza stone, place it inside the oven while preheating to ensure a crispier crust.

Step 3 - Spread the Ketchup Base

Using the back of a spoon or a pastry brush, evenly spread ½ cup of ketchup over the surface of the dough, leaving about a ½-inch border around the edges for the crust. Make sure the layer is thin but fully covers the center to avoid a dry bite.

Step 4 - Add the Cheese

Sprinkle 1 ½ cups of shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the ketchup base. Spread it evenly so that every bite is cheesy and delicious. For extra cheesiness, you can mix in other cheeses like provolone or cheddar.

Step 5 - Add the Pineapple and Other Toppings

Evenly distribute 1 cup of pineapple chunks across the pizza. For a perfect balance of flavor, ensure that the pineapple pieces are not too large and are well spread out. If desired, add other toppings like ham, bacon, bell peppers, or a light sprinkle of dried oregano for extra flavor.

Step 6 - Bake the Pizza

Carefully place the pizza into the preheated oven. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden brown. If using a pizza stone, bake for about 10-12 minutes for a crispier texture.

Step 7 - Bake the Pizza

Once baked, remove the pizza from the oven and let it rest for 2-3 minutes to allow the cheese to set. Use a pizza cutter to slice it into even portions. Serve hot and enjoy!

Ingredients

1 pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)

1 cup pineapple chunks

1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Optional: ham, bacon, bell peppers, oregano

Tools

Baking sheet or pizza stone

Rolling pin

Spoon (for spreading ketchup)

Knife (for slicing toppings)

Pizza cutter
