Cannoli al Cioccolato

A decadent twist on a Sicilian classic, Cannoli al Cioccolato takes the crisp, golden shell of traditional cannoli and fills it with rich, creamy Nutella and soft mini marshmallows. Finished with a delicate dusting of powdered sugar, this indulgent treat is the perfect blend of crunch, chocolate, and melt-in-your-mouth sweetness.

Preparation Time

5 Minutes

Make Time

5 Minutes

Serving Size

6 Cannoli

Chocolate Sweet No Bake Italian Style

Instructions

Step 1 - Prepare the Cannoli Shells

Use 6 store-bought cannoli shells for convenience, ensuring they are crisp and ready to be filled.

Step 2 - Fill with Nutella and Marshmallows

Spoon ½ cup of Nutella into a piping bag (or a ziplock bag with the corner cut off). Pipe the Nutella into each cannoli shell, filling them about halfway. Add mini marshmallows into the center of each cannoli, pushing them gently into the Nutella. Continue piping Nutella until the shell is completely filled.

Step 3 - Dust with Powdered Sugar

Generously dust the filled cannoli with powdered sugar for a classic finishing touch.

Step 4 - Serve and Enjoy

Serve immediately for a crispy bite or let them sit for a few minutes to allow the marshmallows to slightly soften inside the filling. Enjoy your Cannoli al Cioccolato with a cup of espresso (or a glass of milk, if you're feeling extra indulgent).

Ingredients

6 store-bought cannoli shells

½ cup Nutella

½ cup mini marshmallows

Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Tools

Piping bag or ziplock bag

Spoon for filling

Sifter for powdered sugar
