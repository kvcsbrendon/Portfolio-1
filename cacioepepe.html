Cacio e Pepe Americano

A true Roman classic, reinvented for the modern palate. This luxurious dish features perfectly cooked pasta tossed in a rich, velvety jarred Alfredo sauce, finished with a generous sprinkle of pre-grated Parmesan and a bold kick of black pepper. A dish so authentic, you’ll swear it came straight from the streets of Rome.

Preparation Time

5 Minutes

Total Time

15 Minutes

Serving Size

2-4 Servings

Pasta Cheesy Creamy Italian

Instructions

Step 1 - Cook the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add 250g of spaghetti or fettuccine and cook according to package instructions (or slightly longer for a softer texture). Once done, drain the pasta, reserving ½ cup of pasta water for later.

Step 2 - Heat the Alfredo Sauce

In a large pan over low heat, pour in 1 cup of jarred Alfredo sauce. Stir occasionally until warmed through. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water to loosen it.

Step 3 - Toss the Pasta in the Sauce

Add the drained pasta directly to the pan with the Alfredo sauce. Using tongs, toss the pasta until fully coated in the creamy, decadent sauce. If needed, add more pasta water to achieve the perfect consistency.

Step 4 - Add the "Cacio e Pepe" Touch

Generously sprinkle ½ cup of pre-grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta, stirring well to combine. Finish with a heavy dose of black pepper (the more, the better) to give it that true Cacio e Pepe Americano feel.

Step 5 - Serve and Enjoy.

Plate the pasta while warm, topping it with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan and black pepper for that final gourmet touch. Serve immediately and enjoy your authentic Italian masterpiece.

>

Ingredients

250g spaghetti or fettuccine

1 cup jarred Alfredo sauce

½ cup pre-grated Parmesan cheese

1 tsp black pepper (or more to taste)

½ cup reserved pasta water (if needed)

Tools

Large pot for boiling pasta

Strainer for draining pasta

Large pan for heating sauce

Tongs for tossing pasta
