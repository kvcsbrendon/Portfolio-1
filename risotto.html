Risotto Rosso

A modern twist on a timeless Italian dish, Risotto Rosso takes the comforting creaminess of risotto and elevates it with a rich, tangy ketchup infusion. Tender rice, infused with bold tomato sweetness, is finished with vibrant green peas for an authentic touch. This dish is perfect for those who appreciate tradition—with a little extra flair.

Preparation Time

5 Minutes

Make Time

25 Minutes

Serving Size

2-4 Servings

Creamy Ketchup Peas Italian

Instructions

Step 1 - Prepare the Ingredients

Measure out 1 cup of Arborio rice, 3 cups of water or broth, ½ cup of ketchup, and ½ cup of frozen peas. Keep everything within reach for smooth cooking.

Step 2 - Cook the Rice

In a large pan over medium heat, add 1 tbsp of butter or oil. Pour in the Arborio rice and stir it for 1-2 minutes, allowing it to toast slightly.

Step 3 - Add the Liquid

Gradually add 3 cups of water or broth, one cup at a time, stirring constantly to help the rice release its starch. Cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring frequently, until the mixture reaches a thick, gloopy consistency.

Step 4 - Stir in the Ketchup

Once the risotto has absorbed most of the liquid, reduce the heat to low and stir in ½ cup of ketchup. Mix well until the rice is evenly coated in the rich, red sauce.

Step 5 - Add the Peas

Toss in ½ cup of frozen peas, stirring them into the risotto. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the peas are heated through.

Step 6 - Serve and Enjoy

Plate the Risotto Rosso while warm. Optionally, garnish with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese or black pepper for extra depth of flavor. Enjoy your authentic Italian experience with every bite!

Ingredients

1 cup Arborio rice

3 cups water or broth

½ cup ketchup

½ cup frozen peas

1 tbsp butter or oil

Optional: grated Parmesan, black pepper

Tools

Large pan or pot

Wooden spoon for stirring

Measuring cups
