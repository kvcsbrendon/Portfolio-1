Pesto Verde Moderno

A health-conscious reimagining of the traditional pesto, this vibrant green sauce blends fresh spinach with creamy mayonnaise and crunchy walnuts, creating a smooth and indulgent texture. Tossed with perfectly soft penne, Pesto Verde Moderno is a dish that brings a fresh twist to a beloved classic—lighter, smoother, and definitely better for you.

Preparation Time

5 Minutes

Make Time

20 Minutes

Serving Size

2-4 Servings

Pesto Pasta Healthy Modern-Italian

Instructions

Step 1 - Cook the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add 250g of penne pasta and cook 2-3 minutes longer than al dente for a soft, comforting texture. Drain and set aside, reserving ¼ cup of pasta water in case you need to loosen the sauce.

Step 2 -

In a blender or food processor, combine:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach (washed and dried)
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup walnuts
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
Blend until smooth, adding a splash of pasta water if needed for a looser consistency.

Step 3 - Toss the Pasta in the Sauce

Transfer the drained pasta to a large bowl and pour the Pesto Verde Moderno over it. Toss thoroughly until every piece is evenly coated in the rich, creamy sauce.

Step 4 - Serve and Enjoy

Plate the pasta and finish with an optional sprinkle of grated Parmesan or extra walnuts for texture. Serve immediately and enjoy your modern, healthier pesto experience.

Ingredients

250g penne pasta

2 cups fresh spinach

½ cup mayonnaise

¼ cup walnuts

1 garlic clove

¼ tsp salt

2 tbsp olive oil

¼ cup reserved pasta water (if needed)

Optional: grated Parmesan for garnish

Tools

Large pot for boiling pasta

Strainer for draining pasta

Blender or food processor

Large mixing bowl
